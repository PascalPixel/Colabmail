Email is the heart of any business. Productivity tools, to do lists, calendar, Evernote, they all make collaboration easier, but can only compliment and not replace email. There are many solutions out there to manage email in a more efficient way, but they all revolve around the way you prioritize and respond to email, ignoring the fact that what makes your inbox bulky most of the time is the need to cc and fwd email in order to respond to an issue or set up a meeting. ColabMail focuses on the need to collaborate via email while keeping your inbox lean.

The problem

Email is the ultimate task list. Each and every email is one task that need to be discussed, optimized and finally executed by your team. But how can someone collaborate on an email? Most users just forward an email to their colleagues, then they create a task in their favorite tool, then they'll discuss the task in Skype and finally at some point the work gets done. This is obviously a messy way to get things done. We believe there is a better way and here is our proposal for the future.

Socializing email

Our purpose is simple. Make email cool as it supposed to be. We want to create an application that will enable users to collaborate straight from their email client. We will start from Gmail and we are going to create a Firefox Addon that will offer:

  • Email sharing with team members
  • Chat inside the thread
  • Annotations and notes inside emails
We believe that those additions can really revolutionize every day life for startups and bigger companies. Processes like customer support, sales and even product development can be more efficient and less time consuming with a tool like that. Think of having the ability to collaborate with your colleagues from inside Gmail and have all this work there for future team members.
