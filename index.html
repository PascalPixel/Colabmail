Mail for teams the right way

Email is the heart of any business. Productivity tools, to do lists, calendar, Evernote, they all make collaboration easier, but can only compliment and not replace email. There are many solutions out there to manage email in a more efficient way, but they all revolve around the way you prioritize and respond to email, ignoring the fact that what makes your inbox bulky most of the time is the need to cc and fwd email in order to respond to an issue or set up a meeting. ColabMail focuses on the need to collaborate via email while keeping your inbox lean.

The problem

Email is the ultimate task list. Each and every email is one task that needs to be discussed, optimized and finally executed by your team. But how can someone collaborate on an email? Most users just forward an email to their colleagues, then they create a task in their favorite tool, then they'll discuss the task in Skype and finally at some point the work gets done. This is obviously a messy way to get things done. We believe there is a better way and here is our proposal for the future.

Socializing email

ColabMail aims to enable users to collaborate straight from their email client. By adding a layer of collaboration on top of an email that needs to be share, ColabMail will offer the possibility for collaborators to have a shared view of the email, annotations and notes on it and a dedicated chat section. We believe that those additions will revolutionize the way startups, bigger companies and freelancers handle collaboration tasks and manage email. Processes like customer support, sales leads, setting up meetings and even product development can become more efficient and less time consuming with ColabMail.

How

We'll start with Gmail by creating a Firefox Addon that will offer:

  • Email sharing with team members
  • Chat inside the thread
  • Annotations and notes inside emails
